Bio

Hongfa is an Assistant Professor at Guizhou University of Finance and Economics. Concurrently, he is working with Prof. Changgen Peng as a postdoc fellow at State Key Laboratory of Public Big Data, Guiyang. His research interests include cryptography and privacy in mechine learning and networks.
