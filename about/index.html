My Detail CV.

Hongfa is an assistant professor at Guizhou University of Finance and Economics. Concurrently, he is working with Prof. Changgen Peng as a postdoc fellow at State Key Laboratory of Public Big Data, Guiyang. He visited Lehigh University from 2017 to 2018. Hongfa earned his PhD and master degree from Guizhou University supervised in 2019 and 2015, respectively. Before that, he graduated from Qiannan Normal College for Nationalities in 2010. His research interests include security & privacy of mechine learning, graph data, genomic data, data opening and sharing, etc..
